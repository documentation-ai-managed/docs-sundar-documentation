## Prototyping in UXPin

UXPin enables you to create high-fidelity interactive prototypes that closely mimic production behavior. Use our visual tools to add interactions, animations, and logic without writing code.

  <Card title="Interactive Flows" icon="arrow-right" href="#interactions">

## Adding Interactions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Elements" icon="mouse-pointer">
    Choose the elements you want to make interactive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Triggers" icon="zap">
    Set up triggers like clicks, hovers, or form submissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Actions" icon="settings">
    Specify what happens when the trigger occurs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Prototype" icon="play">
    Preview and test your interactive prototype.
  </Step>
</Steps>
